About the site

Welcome to Keightyrose's site, one that shows my development over the semester and the passion project I am currently working on. Here I've covered my progress throughout making this website as well as the Vtuber journey I'm embarking on. You will find developer blogs, my internet art progress and my design process for my site.

About me

I am a fourth year game design student majoring in Game Design and Interactive Media. A year ago I started streaming and now I find myself drawn to learning more about Vtubers. I have been learning how to draw and rig my own models as I want to start using a character as an extension of myself.

Other than that I am currently developing a productivity game-app hybrid where players study along with a character whose needs change throughout the study session. It is framed by the Pomodoro technique and will hopefully aid players in their own study methods.
